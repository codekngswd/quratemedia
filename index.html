About

QurateMe is a media platform designed to empower users in putting creativity first. You have full control when you create your work, shouldn't you have the same when you show it to the world? Welcome to your Gallery.

Create

Do what you do best. Lose yourself to express yourself, express yourself to lose yourself in whichever order, you decide. The important thing is that we never compromise.

Cultivate

Collect, gather, and grow. QurateMe invites you with your best to only further the platform as a collective. R. Buckminster Fuller once said, "I am not a genius. I'm just a tremendous bundle of experience." Come grow with us.

Curate

Show the world what you're about, what you stand for, what your passions are. What drives your day and makes you tick. QurateMe is about furthering that experience through a sense of community. QurateMe is as much about you as an individual as it is about you as a collective unit.

Curate

The underlying philosophy of QurateMe is simplicity in curation - ridding of distractions that take the focus away from your work. The idea has always been to make it easier to have your work seen in a fun, engaging, and aesthetically pleasing manner. We achieve this with a clean user interface, designed in a minimalist fashion that highlights your creations in their full glory.
